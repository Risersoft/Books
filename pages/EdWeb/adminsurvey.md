# Survey
## Create Survey
To create a survey, navigate **Admin > Survey > New Survey**

![](/images/18.png)

 Fill all the details and **Save**

![](/images/19.png)

## List of Surveys

To view all surveys, navigate **Admin > Survey > List of Surveys**.

![](/images/20.png)

**List of Surveys** created by the Admin will appear

![](/images/21.png)
